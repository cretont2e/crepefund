Products Introduction

CrW™

CrW™ (Crepe Work) is a blockchain version of Exchange Traded Fund (ETF) in the traditional financial market. Like an ETF, it is a kind of crypto product that strictly tracks the particular index.

Learn More

CrD™

CrD™ (Crepe Direct Indexing) is a blockchain version of Direct Indexing (DI) in the traditional financial market. CrD™ can be designed, created, and operated by anyone who pays a small deposit and token issuance fee.

Learn More

Algo

DAO-certified investment product managers combine their experience and expertise to develop investment strategies and algorithmize them into products. Various Algo products are traded on the market, allowing users to buy and sell market product tokens.

Learn More

Lagrangian Protocol

Lagrangian Protocol seeks consistent, risk-adjusted returns through a delta-neutral strategy, capitalizing on inefficiencies in cryptocurrency market bases and funding rates, while maintaining portfolio stability.

Learn More
